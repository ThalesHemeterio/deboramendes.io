Debora Mendes

Débora Mendes is a 24-year-old-woman, Brazilian, graduated in Journalism. As a good listener, her passion for photography started listening to history’s from real people, families.

Since Debora has started listening to people, her love for photography grow up. Then Débora begin reporting cases, affections, and good moments through clicks. Real people, with real feelings and real lives. The daily lifestyle has a delicate tone of everyday life. Families, events, loves, and dreams.

As Débora says “…images are instruments to make eternal memories and experiences worth keeping in mind, heart and in an album…”. From the centre of Brazil to Ireland, Debora is ready to record the best moments in your life , and you can keep your memories in a photography.
